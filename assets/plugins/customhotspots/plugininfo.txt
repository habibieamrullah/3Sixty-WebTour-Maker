Custom Hotspots|1.0.0|1.1.0|Habibie (webappdev.id)|https://webappdev.id/|
Enables you to use any image file as a 64 by 64 pixels custom hotspot icon. It will add a custom Hotspot Icons menu button on your project sidebar. Click it to see your custom hotspot image gallery. Add any image to it and use it as your custom hotspot.
